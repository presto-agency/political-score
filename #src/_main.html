About platform

PoliticalScore101 is a unique platform that allows students and professors to create their own debates and encourage civil discussions. Students will have the ability to voice their opinions on curriculum-based discussions with confidence. PoliticalScore101 is designed with the success of students in mind and is especially suited for the ever-growing remote learning environment.

  • Professors and universities

    Professors may create their own debates and assign them to their courses, creating requirements, such as essay length and the minimum of essays each student must vote for.

  • Students

    To encourage students to read and consider the positions of classmates, a professor may require each student to vote for the essays they find the most compelling.
